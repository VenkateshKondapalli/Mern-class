Naming converstion


CamelCase
multiple words aree joined by making the first letter capital (except first word)
ex: help manoj ==> hello-manoj
Kebab-Case/hypen-case
multiple words are joined by '-' in between
ex: help manoj ==> helloManoj
Snake_Case
multiple words aree joined by '_'
ex: help manoj ==> hello_manoj
